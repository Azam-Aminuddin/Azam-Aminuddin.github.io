## Sample: - The non-zero rational number $(\mathbb{Q}*,\times)$ - $(\mathbb{R}*,\times)$ - $(\mathbb{C}*,\times)$ - $(\mathbb{Z}_n, +)$ - $(\mathbb{Z}_p, \times)$ ## Definition - A Set B in a Vector Space V - With element: $ e_1, e_2, ..., e_n $ - Every element $ v \in V $ may be written in a unique way as a linear combination of B - $ v = c_1.e_1 + c_2.e_2 + ... + c_n.e_n $ - $ ( c_1, c_2, ..., c_n ) $ is the coordinates - n is the dimension of the vector space
